About AquaTrace AI

We're on a mission to revolutionize marine species identification and conservation through the power of artificial intelligence and citizen science. Identify over 6500+ marine species with our advanced AI.

🌊 Our Mission

AquaTrace AI is dedicated to making marine research and conservation more accessible to everyone. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with comprehensive marine biodiversity data to provide instant species identification and educational content.

We believe that understanding and protecting marine life starts with knowledge. By empowering researchers, students, and ocean enthusiasts with accurate identification tools, we're building a global community committed to ocean conservation.

Our goal is to create the world's most comprehensive and accessible marine species identification platform, contributing to scientific research and conservation efforts worldwide.

❓ FAQ

How does AquaTrace work?

AquaTrace uses advanced deep learning models trained on thousands of marine species images. Simply upload a photo of a marine creature, and our AI will identify the species with detailed information about habitat, diet, and conservation status.

How is accuracy calculated?

Our model accuracy is calculated using cross-validation on a test dataset of verified marine species images. We achieve 95% accuracy across 6500+ species through continuous training and validation with marine biology experts.
